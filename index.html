let me introduce myself

My name is Sydney. I am a Front-End Web Developer based in Ontario, Canada.

I am currently attending Fanshawe College and completing the Interactive Media Design program. I have found my passion in designing and creating websites using WordPress Customizations. So far, I have worked with Avada and loved every moment of learning all the possible ways I can make a website shine.

I can also code from scratch using Foundations or Bootstrap. When designing, I prefer to use WordPress or Adobe Xd, however I am able to use Photoshop for wireframes as well as Illustrator for vector logos and icons.

During school, I had the opportunity to learn and work with softwares that allowed me to design 3D. Over the summer, my goal is to study and implement 3D into my designs. I am able to use Cinema 4D and After Effects to achieve this goal.
